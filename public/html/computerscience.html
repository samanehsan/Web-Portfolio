A PASSION FOR LEARNING & CREATING

I took my first computer science course during my last year at UVa and my only regret was not taking one sooner. I am inspired by a culture that strives to constantly create and find innovative solutions through computing with an energy that I haven't seen anywhere else. I found that the logical way of thinking about scientific problems easily carries over to CS. I'm interested in the intersection of science and computing, mainly in bioinformatics due to the the very poweful tools and insights it can provide. At the moment, I'm trying to explore all kinds of things CS-related, such as development of mobile and web applications.

Ghost Hunter Android Game

I am working in a team of undergraduate students to develop an Android game called Ghost Hunter for CS2110: Software Development Methods. The objective of the game is to “kill” ghosts as they appear on screen in order to earn points. Our software development practices are loosely based off of the Scrum methodology.
